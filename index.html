About us

Our mission is to connect young pianists and established artists through fair, transparent adjudication and inspiring concert opportunities.

Vision

We celebrate musical excellence and cultural exchange. Winners are invited to a Gala Concert and featured across our media channels.

Format

Preliminary and Final rounds are conducted by video submission. Selected prizewinners perform live at the Winners' Gala Concert.

Rules & regulations

  • Eligibility: Open to pianists of all nationalities (see age categories in full rules).
  • Repertoire: Free choice classical works; one complete movement minimum.
  • Video: Single take preferred; high-quality audio recommended; no edits within a piece.
  • Judging: Blind review when possible; jury decisions are final.
  • Deadlines & Fees: See application portal for current dates and fees.
